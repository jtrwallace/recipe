Volcanic Unicorn Ribeye with Nitro-Gilded Bone Marrow and Celestial Berry Dust

By ChatGPT & Joshua Wallace

Prep Time: 2 days
Cook Time: 2 hours
Total Time: 2 days 2 hours
Servings: 4 servings
Cuisine: Interdimensional Maximalist Fusion

This is not a dish—it’s an experience. A reverse-seared, lava rock-aged ribeye crowned with flambéed bone marrow brushed in edible gold, nestled on a smoked parsnip–potato cloud, with a drizzle of fermented truffle–uni caramel. The plate is finished with liquid nitrogen-frozen berries shattered into iridescent dust and served with a side of glowing yuzu spheres. Eat fast, the plate gets weird.

Ingredients

For the Ribeye

  • 2 18-oz ribeye steaks, dry-aged at home with black lava salt
  • 2 tsp edible gold flakes
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp rendered duck fat
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 4 garlic cloves, smashed

For the Bone Marrow

  • 2 canoe-cut beef marrow bones
  • 1 tbsp cognac
  • 1 tsp chili honey
  • 1 sheet edible gold leaf

For the Potato-Parsnip Cloud

  • 2 large Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled
  • 2 parsnips, peeled
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Liquid smoke (just a drop)

For the Truffle–Uni Caramel

  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
  • ½ tsp uni paste
  • ½ tsp black truffle oil
  • Pinch of Maldon salt

For the Celestial Berry Dust

  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1 tbsp edible glitter (silver or pearlescent)
  • Liquid nitrogen (for freezing & shattering)

For the Glowing Yuzu Spheres

  • 1 cup yuzu juice
  • 1 tsp calcium lactate
  • 1 tsp sodium alginate
  • LED plate (or glowing ice cube, if cheating)

Instructions

  1. Day Before

    1. Dry-age the steaks at home on a rack in the fridge with lava salt for 24 hours.
    2. Freeze the berries in liquid nitrogen and immediately shatter them in a blender with edible glitter. Store in freezer.

  2. Prepare the Spheres

    1. Blend yuzu juice with calcium lactate.
    2. In a separate bowl, mix cold water with sodium alginate.
    3. Drop yuzu mixture in droplets into the alginate bath. Rinse spheres in cold water and store chilled.

  3. Make the Bone Marrow

    1. Roast marrow bones at 450°F (232°C) for 20 minutes.
    2. Drizzle with cognac and chili honey, flambé with a blowtorch, then gently brush with gold leaf.

  4. Make the Truffle–Uni Caramel

    1. Melt sugar with water over medium heat until amber.
    2. Off heat, whisk in uni paste and truffle oil. Add Maldon salt. Keep warm.

  5. Make the Potato–Parsnip Cloud

    1. Boil potatoes and parsnips, then mash with butter, cream, and a whisper of liquid smoke. Whip until smooth.

  6. Cook the Ribeye

    1. Sear steaks in duck fat with garlic and rosemary. Finish in 275°F (135°C) oven until medium rare (125°F/52°C). Rest, then hit with blowtorch and edible gold flakes.

  7. Plate the Dish

    1. Smear potato cloud on glowing LED plate. Top with steak and bone marrow. Drizzle with truffle–uni caramel. Dust with shattered celestial berry dust. Garnish with yuzu spheres and edible flowers.
