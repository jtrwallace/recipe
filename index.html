Chorizo & Egg Breakfast Burrito with Onions May 12, 2025

A bold, savory breakfast classic: smoky chorizo, fluffy eggs, and sweet sautéed onions wrapped in a warm tortilla. Perfectly satisfying on its own or even better with cheese—if you're feeling indulgent.

Author: ChatGPT & Joshua Wallace

Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 15 min

Total Time: 25 min

Ingredients

Main Filling

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 oz fresh Mexican chorizo (casing removed if necessary)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk or water (for fluffier eggs)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Optional

  • ½ cup shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese (optional)

Assembly

  • 2 large flour tortillas (10-inch)
  • Hot sauce or salsa, to taste
  • Foil or parchment paper, for wrapping (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the Filling

  1. Heat olive oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add sliced onions and sauté until soft and golden, about 5–7 minutes.
  2. Add chorizo to the pan. Break it apart with a spatula and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 5 minutes. Stir occasionally to mix with the onions.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with milk or water, plus a pinch of salt and pepper. Push chorizo mixture to one side of the skillet, reduce heat to low, and pour in eggs. Scramble gently until just set, then mix with chorizo and onions.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the filling while warm, letting it melt (optional).

Assemble Burritos

  1. Heat tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  2. Spoon half the filling onto each tortilla. Add hot sauce or salsa if desired. Fold in sides and roll tightly into a burrito.
  3. Serve immediately, or wrap in foil for on-the-go.

Servings

2 large burritos

Notes

  1. For a crispier burrito, pan-sear the wrapped burrito on a skillet for 1–2 minutes per side.
  2. Use soy chorizo and plant-based eggs for a vegetarian version.
  3. Extra filling can be refrigerated for up to 3 days—great for quick breakfasts.
