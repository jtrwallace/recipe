Coral Reef Sea Creatures Fried with Avocados

A tropical-inspired seafood dish that combines the delicate flavors of ocean creatures—like shrimp, scallops, and squid—with creamy avocado and a light, crispy batter. Perfect with a citrusy aioli or mango salsa.
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Recipe Category: Main Course
Recipe Cuisine: Tropical Fusion
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 6 sea scallops
  • 1 small squid, cleaned and sliced into rings
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced into wedges
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges (for serving)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 cup sparkling water (cold)
  • Neutral oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Pat shrimp, scallops, and squid dry with paper towels. Set aside.
  2. Cut each avocado in half, remove pit, and slice into thick wedges.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and paprika. Slowly whisk in cold sparkling water until a smooth, thick batter forms.
  4. In a deep pan or pot, heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Dip each seafood piece and avocado wedge into the batter. Fry in batches until golden and crisp (about 2 minutes per batch). Don’t overcrowd the pot. Transfer to a paper towel–lined plate.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with cilantro and lime wedges. Great with a citrus aioli or mango salsa.

Notes

  • You can substitute in reef-safe, sustainable seafood depending on availability.
  • Serve with a side of coconut rice or on top of a tropical salad.
  • For extra crunch, add panko breadcrumbs to the batter.
