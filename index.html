Spaghetti Carbonara

A simple and delicious spaghetti carbonara recipe.

Author

JTech AI

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti.
  2. Cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese.
  4. Combine everything and mix quickly off heat.
  5. Serve with more cheese and pepper.
