Cow Dung Burritos

A hilariously disgusting-looking dessert burrito that tastes like fudgy heaven.

Ingredients

  • 4 small flour tortillas (6–8 inch)
  • 1 cup chocolate fudge brownie mix (prepared and baked)
  • 1/2 cup Nutella or chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 1/2 cup crushed Oreos (for “soil clumps”)
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup mini marshmallows (optional)
  • Chocolate syrup (for smearing)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, crumble the baked brownie into chunks. Mix with Nutella, crushed Oreos, peanut butter, and chocolate chips. Stir in marshmallows if using.
  2. Microwave tortillas for 10–15 seconds to soften.
  3. Spoon filling into each tortilla and roll into a burrito.
  4. Smear chocolate syrup and extra Oreo crumbles on top for realism.
  5. Serve and enjoy the prank.
